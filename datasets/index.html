How To Read The Status Column

Internal rows means the result came from CALB itself. It is useful, but it is not external validation. No support means the frozen external adapter ran and failed the rule or failed to beat the baseline. Blocked means the required paired evidence is missing. Partial support means the locked supervised model beat a boring baseline, but not by enough to call it strong external support.
