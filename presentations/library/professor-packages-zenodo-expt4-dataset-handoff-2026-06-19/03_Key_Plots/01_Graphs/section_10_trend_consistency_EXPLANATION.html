Graph Explanation: EIS 2D DRT surface Trend Consistency

Graph file: section_10_trend_consistency.png

What This Figure Shows

This graph shows per-cell Spearman correlations between fitted features and ageing labels.

How To Read It

Stable colors across cells mean the feature moves consistently as cells age. Random sign flips mean the feature is not reliable enough to treat as a health indicator.

What We Can Learn

R0 is expected to be more stable than individual DRT band features. If DRT bands do not trend consistently, say so.

Caveat

Five RPT points per cell is small. This is a sanity check, not a final statistical proof.
