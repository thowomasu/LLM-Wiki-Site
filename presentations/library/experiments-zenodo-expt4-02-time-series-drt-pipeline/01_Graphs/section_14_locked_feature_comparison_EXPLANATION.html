Graph Explanation: Section 14 Locked Feature Comparison

Graph file: section_14_locked_feature_comparison.png

What This Figure Shows

This graph compares locked R0-only, DRT-band-only, and R0-plus-DRT-band feature sets.

How To Read It

The two panels are leave-one-cell-out and leave-one-temperature-out validation. If R0 plus DRT bands does not beat R0 only, the DRT bands are not adding a defensible health signal.

What We Can Learn

This is the sharpest DRT-specific benchmark in the package.

Caveat

This is still internal health-label validation, not EIS-derived DRT validation. R0 itself is a same-test resistance-like feature for the 0.1s resistance target.
